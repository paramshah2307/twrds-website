designed for

Because sometimes, you like to live a little.
And most systems don't really expect that.

for the longest time, personal finance apps have been built for made-up people. the kind that are always motivated, always disciplined, and never distracted.

but life isn't designed for ideal versions of us.

life is crazy. imperfect. non-linear.

life is human.

and as a human, you chase the big dreams.

the house, the money, the vacations. as you should.

but every once in a while, you love to splurge on the small things too...

dinner with friends at a place you'll remember,

the best seats at a once-in-a-lifetime concert,

gifts for your parents from that first salary.

And that's okay.

You're a person. Not a spreadsheet.

but most tools today treat you like one.

they force a choice. enjoy in the present or build your future.

you download them anyway, because they promise a fresh start. You're genuinely excited to be the person who finally has their life together.

all of that sounds great, until none of it works anymore.

because nothing in your life actually changes. you open the app, you track, you budget, you follow the plan. and then life happens anyway.

there’s no real reason to come back tomorrow.

no feeling of progress. no reward for showing up.

just the quiet guilt of knowing what you should do,

and the very human choice to do what you want in the moment.

when the solutions meant to help you start standing in your way,

the only choice left is to start over and do it right.


So we built twrds.


twrds is a personal finance app designed to fit you,

and not the other way around.

we’re building a way to stay in the game while enjoying life,

without the constant pressure to get every move right.

the future you want, the life you have.

finally in the same place.
